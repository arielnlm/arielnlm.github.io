Mladen Jovanovic

Computer Scientist

Hello! I'm Mladen Jovanović, born on April 4th, 2000, in Belgrade. My adventure in Computer Science began at the Faculty of Computing in Belgrade for the 2019/2020 academic year. I completed my undergraduate studies in 2023, achieving an impressive average grade of 9.36 and dedicating my thesis to the captivating realm of Genetic Algorithms. That same year, I eagerly continued my journey by enrolling in master's studies to delve deeper into Computer Science.

Currently, I'm also embracing the role of a Teaching Assistant at the University of Belgrade and also have the pleasure of teaching Game Development, merging my passion for education with my love for creating engaging gaming experiences.
